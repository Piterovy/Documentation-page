    • Introduction

    This page is created to assist you in process of provisioning a Polycom VoIP handset. Please do not hesitate if you get stuck on any of the points and contact us on our e-mail address or give us a call, so one of our agents can assist you.

    Below will be required to perform this operation:

    • Polycom VoIP handset
    • Access to internet
    • PoE Switch or power supply for phone
    • Computer (optional for web interface provisioning)
